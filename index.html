FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 1–3 weeks depending on scope. Landing pages can launch in a few days, while full websites with AI chatbot and WhatsApp automation typically take 2–3 weeks.

Do you offer WhatsApp and AI chatbot integration for any business type?

Yes. I've built these systems for mobile stores, restaurants, clinics, and salons, and the same approach adapts well to real estate agencies, car garages, and most other small to medium businesses.

What is included in the price?

Each plan includes design, development, mobile responsiveness, and basic SEO. AI chatbot and WhatsApp automation are included from the Professional plan upward. A detailed breakdown is shared before any work begins.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes. Website Maintenance is available as a standalone service or as an add-on to any plan, covering updates, monitoring, and small content changes.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. I review your current site, identify what's holding it back, and rebuild it with a modern design, better performance, and clearer calls to action — without losing the content that already works.
